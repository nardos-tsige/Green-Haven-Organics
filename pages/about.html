Our Story

Rooted in Ethiopian soil since 2022

In the highlands of Ethiopia, a small group of farmers dreamed of bringing pure, organic food back to city tables. Green Haven was born from that dream — a bridge between traditional farming wisdom and modern sustainable living.

Today we partner with 28 certified organic farms, deliver to over 4,000 homes every week, and plant one tree for every box delivered.
